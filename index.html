JavaScript Minesweeper

JavaScript implementation of the classic game, Minesweeper. Click the squares to reveal what is underneath them. There are mines hidden randomly in the field, and each is surrounded by squares that are numbered to hint at their proximity. Click a mine and it's game over, Right click to place a flag over suspected mines. Flag all mines or reveal all non-mined spaces to win!
